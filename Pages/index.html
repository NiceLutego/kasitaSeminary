ABOUT KASITA SEMINARY

KASITA SEMINARY

Kasita Seminary is an essential place where the church of Mahenge looks after the seeds of vocation sown in the hearts of the young boys.God has planted the seed of vocation to priesthood in the boys entrusted to us by the Bishops and parish priest.Kasita Seminary belongs to the diocese of Mahenge and was built in 1960.It is allocated at 100 Km from Morogoro Municipality.

Kasita seminary is divided into two blocks:

1.ORDINARY LEVEL

Form One

These are our beloved form one students at Kasita Seminary.They are 44 in the class,among these students some are taking Pure Science,Social Science,Fine Art and Amali.

Form Two

These are our mostly beloved candidates of Form Two 2025.They are 45 in the class,the Seminary has entrusted them that they are going to represent the Seminary in FTNA 2025.

Form Three

These are our sub-candidates Form three 2025.They are 40 in the class who passed the FTNA 2024 the whole class,then the Seminary has trusted them that they can proceed with the formation.

Form Four

These are the Candidates Form four 2025.They are 66 in class.The Seminary has the hopes that the Form Four students are going to brand the the name of the Seminary National wise in CSEE 2025.

2.ADVANCED LEVEL

Form Five

These are Form Five students at Kasita Seminary.They 8 in the class,this number include both Form Five Arts and Form Five Science.

Form Six

These are our mostly beloved Form six Students at Kasita Seminary.They are 29 in the class,this class combine both Form six Science and Form Six Arts.

OUTSIDE ACADEMIC ASPECT

Work

Our Seminary doesn't form the Seminarians only in Academic aspects but also in the aspect of Work,in order to make them capable of being self-reliant in their future life of Priesthood.

Prayers

Our beloved Seminarians are mostly formed to know God and Worship him.They extremely formed spiritually in order to make them trustful and the agents of God after they become priests as well as Preaching the Word of God.

Sports

One among the qualities of the Seminarians is to be strong and healthy,our Seminary gives the priority to the aspect of sports and games in order for them to be healthy physically and mentally.

Disciplinary

The most important thing that a seminarian must have is self-discipline this means that the Seminarian must be able to control his emotions and actions,hence our Seminary has given this aspect the great priority in order to make sure that all Seminarians are self-disciplined inside and outside the Seminary.

DEPARTMENTS

PHYSICS

This is the department that deals with the Physics Subject.It is responsible for all issues concerning Physics Subject aspecially in coordination of the Physics Examinations in supervising and marking the Exams after being done.

LANGUAGE

This is the department which is concerned with the Language and Linguistics issues here at Kasita Seminary.This department deals with three subjects which are English,Kiswahili and French Language.

MATHEMATICS

This is the department that deals with the Mathematics subjects in both levels(Ordinary level and Advanced Level).It is responsible for coordination of all Mathematics Examinations here at Kasita Seminary.

CHEMISTRY

This is a Scientific department that deals with Chemistry Subject here at Kasita Seminary.It has the Teachers who are experts in this Science of Chemicals.It is responsible in coordination of all issues concerning Chemistry Subject including Exams and different projects.

HISTORY

This is the department that deals with the History subject here at Kasita Seminary.It has enough Teachers teaching History in both Ordinary level(Form I-Form IV) and Advanced Level(Form V and Form VI).

COMPUTER SCIENCE

This is the Technological department that deals and handle all Technological issues at Kasita Seminary.This department is now playing a big role in most of aspects such as controling information systems and Printing activities.Apart from these roles it is responsible for teaching Computer Science Subject.

HISTORIA YA TANZANIA

This is the department that deals with the Tanzania History Subject here at Kasita Seminary.It is newly found department since it has started last year.It is responsible for all matters concerning this Subject.

GEOGRAPHY

This is department that deals with Geographical matters here at Kasita Seminary.It has enough teachers teaching Geography in both Ordianry level(I-IV) and Advanced Level(V-VI).

BIBLE KNOWLEDGE

This is very crucial department here in the Seminary since it helps the Seminary to teach the Seminarians concerning God news.It is the department that has experts of bible who are teaching the Subject including Priests,Deacons and teachers.

BIOLOGY

This is scientific department that deals with Biological issues here at Kasita Seminary.This department is responsible for teaching Biology Subject in both O'level and A'level of education.
